Escape the Dark Web — Rules & Regulations

A cyber-security challenge.

Event Details

  • 1 member per team.
  • Time limit: 15 minutes.
  • The event involves finding clues, solving puzzles, and escaping before time runs out.
  • The concepts are based on Computer Science and Cyber Security.

Rules

  • Mobile phones, calculators, or any external help are strictly not allowed.
  • No physical force is needed. Do not break props, locks, or objects.

Guidelines

  • Clues may be hidden in images, texts, objects, or riddles.
  • The first person those who fill the form first will be the first winner.
