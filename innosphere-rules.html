INNOSPHERE — Rules & Regulations

Innovate, Build, and Showcase.

Project Categories

  • Open to all UG & PG Students.
  • Projects can be hardware prototypes, IoT devices, or innovative software solutions.
  • A live Demo of your project is mandatory.
  • Your Own Laptops will be Allowed
  • Participants are required to bring their own hardware components.
  • Be ready for jury question for after demo.

Themes

  1. Smart & Sustainable Living
    Innovations in renewable energy, smart homes, eco-friendly devices, sustainable agriculture.
  2. Healthcare & Well-being Technologies
    Wearables, AI-driven diagnostics, assistive devices, telemedicine platforms.
  3. AI, Robotics & Intelligent Automation
    Autonomous robots, drones, AI-powered tools, industry automation solutions.
  4. Education, Security & Social Impact
    EdTech platforms, cybersecurity tools, fintech solutions, rural development & accessibility tech.

Team Structure

  • Teams must consist of a maximum of 3 members.

Presentation Guidelines

  • Each team will be given 8-10 minutes for their presentation and demo.
  • Presentations should be between 7 slides in length.
