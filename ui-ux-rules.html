UI/UX DESIGN — Rules & Regulations

Innovate with great design.

Participants & Time

  • Open to all UG & PG Students.
  • 1 member per team.
  • 10 minutes will be allotted for understanding the problem statement before the design phase begins.
  • The main design phase has a time limit of 40 minutes.

Task Description

  • A problem statement (e.g., sample website or application) will be given on the spot.
  • Participants must redesign the interface** to make it more user-friendly, innovative, and to improve flow.
  • The design should demonstrate the participant's creativity.

Rules & Tools

  • Tools allowed: Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, Canva, or any other design tool.
  • Internet access is allowed for design assets, icons, and inspiration.
  • No phones or AI tools are allowed during the competition.
