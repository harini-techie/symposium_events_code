AR & VR — Rules & Regulations

Experience Reality in a New Way.

Team & Time

  • Open to all UG & PG Students.
  • Teams must consist of 2 members.
  • The time limit for the event is 1:30 hours.
  • Your Own Laptops will be Allowed

Task Description

  • A demo AR game preview will be provided.
  • Participants must recreate a similar AR game using only Unity Hub.
  • The final game must be built and shown running on the participants' own mobile phones.

Rules & Scoring

  • Internet usage is allowed only for reference, not for downloading pre-made projects.
  • Additional features such as a timer, UI score, or animations are optional but will be awarded extra credit.
